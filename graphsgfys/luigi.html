Luigi Rising Cyclone Heights

This gfycat shows the various heights Luigi can gain from his rising cyclone by mashing the b button during the animation.

Each #Press represents a cyclone with that amount of B presses within the animation (mashing window is between frame 6-43, and the whole animation lasts 79 frames). Luigi is shown on the first frame of his doublejump which is performed the first frame possible after cyclone. This allows us to see the true height gained from the starting position.

Frame 6 of the cyclone is also labeled, this is because his cyclone has a downward movement at the start until frame 6, Luigi cannot mash during this time.

To perform a rising cyclone, Luigi must first perform a cyclone on the ground. This is known as charging, and the exact details of this can be found by clicking more info.
